Healthcare Access Challenges in Africa

Many people in Africa face limited access to quality healthcare due to remote locations, a shortage of specialists, high travel costs, and long waiting times at medical centers.

  • Geographic distance from medical facilities
  • Lack of specialized healthcare professionals
  • High costs and time required for travel
  • Long waiting periods for consultations

Our Telemedicine Solution

TibaGo connects patients with certified healthcare professionals remotely via mobile and web platforms, enabling medical consultations, advice, prescriptions, and personalized health follow-up without the need to travel.

  • Remote consultations via mobile and web
  • Access to certified medical professionals
  • Personalized health monitoring and follow-up
  • Convenient and cost-effective healthcare access
